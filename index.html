bio

Born in Chicago, Illinois, raised in Phoenix, Arizona, Roman Valadez is a director and visual artist. Beginning at 20 with no formal training, Valadez built his practice from the ground up — shooting artists, learning every aspect of production, and developing a style rooted in visceral emotion and high energy. His aesthetic draws from surrealism, dark imagery, and cinematic precision, combining these disciplines into a body of work that is visceral, intentional, and distinctly his own.
