bio

ROMANBEROAMIN (Roman Valadez) is a director and visual artist.

Chicago. Phoenix. Los Angeles.

Roman picked up a camera at 17 and built his practice from the ground up — across every stage of production. His work combines film, VFX, editing, sound design, and 3D animation into full multimedia experiences, rooted in surrealism, dark imagery, and cinematic precision. Driven by faith and craft, he creates work that is visceral, intentional, and distinctly his own.
